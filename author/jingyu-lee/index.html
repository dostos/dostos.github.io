I’m a computer science PhD student at the Human-Centered Computer Systems Lab at Seoul National University under supervision of professor Youngki Lee. My current research focuses on mobile and edge computing towards ubiquitous computing system.

I received my masters at Deep Learning and Image Processing Group in Computer Graphics and Image Processing Lab about medical image processing and deep learning.

I graduated from DigiPen Institute of Technology with a B.S in Computer Science in Real-Time Interactive Simulation and worked on Minecraft: Education Edition at Microsoft Studios as a software engineer.
